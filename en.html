Design is my language, my craft, my way of seeing the world. Every project, every stroke, every pixel, and every form is a reflection of something within me — an idea, a feeling, a quiet moment translated into form that belongs uniquely to me. I do not simply create to follow patterns, but to carve my own and redefine.

My name is Arkhat, and I am a 15-year-old graphic and UI/UX designer. My work is shaped by curiosity and an endless pursuit of expression. I experiment with styles, mediums, and approaches, yet always keep something unmistakably mine in every piece I make. Whether it’s a digital interface, a conceptual graphic, or an identity design, I put soul into my work — gracious yet bold, intentional yet fluid.

Each project is an exploration, a dialogue between structure and intuition. Some are meticulous, others raw and instinctive, but all hold a piece of me. My portfolio is not just a collection of works, it is a living archive of my evolution as a designer — one that will continue to grow, shift, and take new forms.

I create because I must. Because design is not just what I do, it is who I am. And this is only the beginning.
